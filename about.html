How can networking platfrom help in women empowerment?

A networking platform can significantly contribute to women's empowerment by providing a space for women to connect, share experiences, access mentorship opportunities, learn about career advancement paths, build confidence, and gain visibility within their industries, effectively breaking down barriers and promoting equality in the workplace.

Why is Women Empowerment lacking in Nepal?

Lack of Access to Information: In rural areas, many women have limited access to education, internet, and media, which restricts their knowledge about empowerment programs or their rights. Cultural and Social Barriers: Deep-rooted patriarchal norms and traditions often prevent women from stepping out of traditional roles. Many may not even be aware that they have the right to make independent choices. Ineffective Implementation of Policies: Although there are laws and policies promoting gender equality, poor implementation means the benefits rarely reach those in need. Limited Representation: Women’s voices in politics, leadership, and decision-making positions are still underrepresented. Without strong role models, many women remain unaware of their potential. Economic Dependence: Financial dependence on male family members can restrict women from seeking opportunities, making empowerment seem like a distant concept. Fear of Backlash: Some women may be aware of their rights but hesitate to speak up due to fear of social backlash or family pressure.
